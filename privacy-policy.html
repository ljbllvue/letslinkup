We are the data controller under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. What personal data we collect

When you use our platform, we may collect the following data:

  • Form Submissions: collaboration intents, scene ideas, preferences, availability
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device ID, referrer URL.
  • Cookies and Usage Data: page views, click behavior, scroll depth, time spent.
  • Sensitive Information: only if you explicitly choose to share it — for example, types of sexual activities mentioned in your forms.

We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18 years old.

3. Why we collect and use your data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To publish your Link Up.
  • To match you with other creators.
  • To communicate with you (e.g. replies, confirmations).
  • To improve our service and user experience.
  • To send updates or promotional messages (only with your consent).
  • To prevent abuse and ensure community safety.

We only process your data when we have a legal basis — for example, when it's necessary to provide the service, when you give us consent, or when we have a legitimate interest that doesn't override your rights.

4. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to help the site function, remember your preferences, and analyze how the platform is used.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. We don't use invasive tracking or third-party ads.

5. Who we share your data with

We do not sell your data. We may share limited information with:

  • Other creators you've chosen to match with.
  • Our trusted service providers (e.g. web hosting, analytics tools).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

Some of our providers may be based outside the European Economic Area. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses).

6. How long we keep your data

We keep your data only as long as necessary:

  • Matchmaking forms are kept for 6 to 12 months.
  • Emails or messages are kept as long as needed for legal or safety reasons.

You can request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct or delete your data.
  • Limit or object to certain uses.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Request a copy of your data (data portability).
  • File a complaint with the French data protection authority (CNIL).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: contact@softlylinked.net

We'll respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We use internal policies and manual moderation.

Please use strong passwords and keep your login information safe.

9. Age restrictions

This platform is only for adults aged 18 and over.
By using it, you confirm you are at least 18. We do not knowingly collect any data from minors.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If the changes are significant, we'll notify you via a banner on the website.
